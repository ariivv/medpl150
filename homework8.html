ABOUT MY GAME

My character's name is Lily. She's from New York, but she is visiting London for spring break. She's very excited to do touristy things and see landmarks like the London Eye and Big Ben. To get there though, she needs to take the tube. How hard can that be? It must be just like the subway system back at home right? Can you help Lily get to the landmarks before it gets dark?
